**Defined:** 2026-01-18
**Core Value:** Risk must be required for progress. No tedious grinding to avoid challenge.

## v1.1 Requirements

### Drowning Modification

- [x] **DROWN-01**: Drowning damage rate reduced to 4 seconds (from 1 second)

### Spear Removal

- [x] **SPEAR-01**: Player cannot craft any spear (all 7 tiers disabled)
- [x] **SPEAR-02**: Spears do not appear in structure loot chests
- [x] **SPEAR-03**: Mobs that spawn with spears do not drop them on death

### Projectile Combat

- [x] **PROJ-01**: Player projectile hit applies Speed II to target mob for 6 seconds
- [x] **PROJ-02**: Player projectile hit applies Glowing to target mob for 6 seconds
- [x] **PROJ-03**: Player projectile hit redirects mob aggro to the shooter
- [x] **PROJ-04**: Player projectiles travel 20% faster initially (velocity boost on launch)
- [x] **PROJ-05**: Player projectiles experience increased gravity after traveling 8 blocks
